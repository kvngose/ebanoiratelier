The Studio

Design Beyond
Aesthetics

Founded by Sarah Oraifoh, Ebanoir Atelier is a contemporary interior design studio dedicated to creating spaces that feel intentional, refined, and deeply personal. Based in Nigeria, the studio specializes in interior design, renovation, and interior styling.

Every project is approached with a balance of functionality, elegance, and storytelling — transforming ordinary spaces into experiences that reflect the lifestyle, personality, and vision of each client.

With a design language rooted in modern elegance, clean lines, warmth, and timeless detail, Ebanoir Atelier creates interiors that are both visually striking and livable.

— Sarah Oraifoh, Founder
What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive design solutions for every space and aspiration.

01

Interior Design

Full-service interior design for residential, commercial, and hospitality spaces. From concept to completion, we craft environments that feel extraordinary and effortlessly livable.

Renovation

How We Work

The Design Journey

01

Discovery

We begin with an in-depth conversation to understand your lifestyle, preferences, needs, and vision. This forms the foundation of everything we create together.

02

Concept Design

We translate your brief into a detailed concept — mood boards, spatial layouts, material palettes, and 3D visualizations that bring the vision to life before a single nail is hammered.

03

Execution

We manage procurement, contractors, and timelines with precision. Every detail is supervised to ensure the finished space matches — or exceeds — the original concept.

04

Reveal

The final reveal is a moment of transformation. We style, photograph, and hand over a space that feels completely yours — refined, personal, and built to last.
